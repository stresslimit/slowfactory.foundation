How we work

Slow Factory has been operating education, design and community initiatives in the fashion sector since 2013
working with partners including global brands, nonprofits and academia.

Work with us

Our purpose-driven initiatives drive our social and environmental mission at a global scale, enabled by a range of partnerships. These projects focus on specific issues and industries, galvanizing action in the industry.

These relationships create opportunities for corporations to develop deeper transparency and innovation strategies, leading to increased brand awareness and loyalty in the social impact space.

By taking real action toward social and environmental justice, organizations and brands can do better business without compromising their values.
