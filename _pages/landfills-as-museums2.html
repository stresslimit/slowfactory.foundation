Why linear is bad—
Most production systems today are linear—starting from design to product creation, marketing and sales, many companies end their thinking there. Waste is often not incorporated into their processes or resources. A product is therefore created without considering its end of life, which often causes the item to be discarded and end up in a landfill.

  • What is a waste-led design system?
    Waste-led design is a practice in which a product's end of life is considered from the beginning of the design process rather than the end. This allows for waste to be a core part of the process just as functionality, material, and comfort are.
