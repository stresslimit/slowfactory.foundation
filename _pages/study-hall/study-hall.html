An Open Conference on Climate & Culture, using fashion as medium for social & environmental change

International and interdisciplinary seminars from industry leaders, scientists, advocates, artists & elected officials

Study Hall is an education initiative focused on climate & culture, using fashion as medium for social & environmental change. The cross-cultural, inter-disciplinary global conferences convene throughout the year, fostering partnerships and education around sustainability literacy in Fashion and beyond.

Each event gathers hundreds of industry leaders, scientists, advocates & influencers. We prioritize and center the voices of Black, Brown, Indigenous and other marginalized voices.
