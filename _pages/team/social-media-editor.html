Social Media Manager & Researcher

We strongly encourage Black Brown Indigenous and Minority Ethnic people to apply for this role. The work Slow Factory does at the intersection of climate justice and human rights is deeply anchored in decoloniality and requires the perspective of people from the Global Majority.

How you will contribute

  • Be the principal manager of Slow Factory social media platform
  • Coordinate with the content team to curate, write, design and post. Our team includes writers, researchers, editors and designers and you will be a keystone in gathering, reviewing and scheduling content to post
  • Contribute to content strategy and posting schedule — review the yearly calendar and upcoming important cultural milestones.
  • Research and write content [to be designed by our team], captions, tweets, for primarily IG and twitter
  • Read and moderate the community: overseeing comments and discussions, replying in a deeply considered way, making sure the overall community we are creating is positive, progressive and focused on social and environmental change
  • Visual design skills are a plus not not necessary
  • Video editing a plus

What we are looking for

  • Social media immersed — finger on the pulse of “what’s happening” on social and relevant cultural movements
  • A natural leader with the self-assurance to gather disparate creative talents around a clear plan
  • Strong communication skills and the ability to navigate conversations with sensitivity and empathy
  • Credibility and positionality on pertinent conversations on racial, economic, environmental and societal inequalities
  • Well-versed and immersed in the sustainability world
  • Hyper detail-oriented
  • Entrepreneurial
  • Takes initiative and values collaboration
  • Not afraid to ask questions
  • Strong understanding of the nuances, cultural history and positions of the complex social, racial and socio-economic realities in the US and abroad
  • Research and writing is required
  • Great writing & wordsmith qualities
  • Community-centered & fluent in social justice issues in the US and Internationally

Why work with us

Using a holistic, equity-centered approach, we bridge science, human rights, technology, fashion & culture to develop products, resources and certifications for industry and consumers alike.

Our team of experts come from varied and creative backgrounds including journalism, trend forecasting, design, technology, and more. As a team, we value collaboration, an open-mind, respectfulness, compassion, and communication.

The opportunity to think in systems outside of white supremacy and colonialism for the sole purpose of creatively and intentionally dismantling these very systems of oppression is a unique invitation.

Slow Factory is a community and a family where you can come to work as yourself, where the work itself is a collective healing process allowing you to blossom, expand in your areas of expertise and be constantly inspired by your team members.

Our team is made of super heroes—and we think you’d be a great addition to work on big projects and opportunities to impact this world in a positive way for generations to come.

We invite you to bridge your values with your everyday actions and join a team doing important work the world, our communities and Mother Earth deeply needs.
